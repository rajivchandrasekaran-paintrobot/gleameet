GleaMeet Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

GleaMeet is a private, real-time AI meeting coach for Google Meet. Your privacy is fundamental to how we build this product. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the controls you have.

1. Data We Collect

When you enable coaching during a Google Meet call, GleaMeet may capture:

  • Meeting timing — start/end times, talk-time ratios, pause durations
  • Speech patterns — filler-word counts, monologue length, question frequency
  • Transcript excerpts — portions of meeting transcripts used for real-time analysis

We also store your Google account email and display name for authentication purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Real-time coaching — speech patterns and transcripts are analyzed during the meeting to deliver private, in-meeting prompts visible only to you.
  • Post-meeting reports — after the meeting, a summary report is generated highlighting strengths and areas for improvement.

Data is processed to provide the coaching service and is never used to train AI models.

3. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed. Default retention periods are:

Data TypeRetention Period
Raw transcripts7 days
Derived features (speech metrics)30 days
Coaching prompts90 days
Meeting reports365 days

After the retention period, data is automatically and permanently deleted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data may be processed by our cloud infrastructure providers (hosting, database) solely to operate the service.

5. Your Rights

  • You can delete your data at any time through the extension settings.
  • You can revoke access by removing the extension from Chrome.
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Session tokens are stored securely and expire after 24 hours. Coaching overlays are injected locally and are never visible to other meeting participants.
