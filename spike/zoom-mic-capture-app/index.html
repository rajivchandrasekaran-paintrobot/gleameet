GleaMeet listens for patterns like these during your meeting.

Persuasion & Influence (Cialdini)

Reciprocity

Detects persuasion without first acknowledging the other side.

Commitment & Consistency

Detects stalled discussions where smaller commitments could help.

Social Proof

Detects resistance when peer examples could reduce uncertainty.

Authority

Detects recommendations without supporting evidence.

Cognitive Bias (Kahneman)

System 1 vs System 2

Detects rapid rebuttal without acknowledgment or clarification.

Loss Aversion

Detects overweighting of downside without balancing upsides.

Anchoring

Detects locking discussion with concrete numbers too early.

Overconfidence

Detects high certainty language with low evidence support.

Behavioral Economics (Thaler)

Choice Architecture

Detects too many options without a recommended default.

Default Effect

Detects stalled group decisions where a clear default could help.

Nudge

Detects agreement without action structure near close.

Present Bias

Detects overweighting immediate friction over future payoff.
